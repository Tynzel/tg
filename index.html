Research Interests

I hold a degree in mathematics and physics with 1st class honours in mathematics from the Australian National University. I did my honours in general relativity under the supervision of Professor Ben Andrews. I am now studying a PhD in the mathematical foundations of string theory under the supervision of Professor Peter Bouwknegt of the ANU and Associate Professor Meng-Chwan Tan of NUS.
